Top 10 List 2024

I am so exited to release my "Top 10 List" for 2024.

These are cubes I recommend with flying colors.

If you like buying new cubes every once and a while but have trouble deciding on a good cube, this is the list for you!

Highest Range: Gan 251M Leap 2x2 (Magnetic UV Coated)
